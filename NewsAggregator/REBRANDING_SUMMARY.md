## Why "Global Pulse"?

1. **Better Readability:** Two words are easier to parse than compound word
2. **Professional Appearance:** More formal than single compound word
3. **Clear Meaning:** "Global" + "Pulse" = distinct concepts combined
4. **Brand Flexibility:** Easier to use in various contexts
5. **International Appeal:** Clear for non-native English speakers
