Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

ContentSphere is a privacy-first news aggregation app. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is handled when you use the app.

Information Collection

ContentSphere does not collect, store, transmit, or sell personal information.

Accounts

ContentSphere does not require account creation or user sign-in.

Analytics and Tracking

ContentSphere does not use analytics SDKs, advertising frameworks, or third-party tracking technologies.

Local Data

Any preferences, bookmarks, or reading activity are stored locally on your device using standard iOS storage mechanisms. This data never leaves your device.

Third-Party Content

ContentSphere displays publicly available content from third-party RSS feeds. Content is shown with attribution and links back to the original publisher. ContentSphere does not claim ownership of third-party content.

Children’s Privacy

ContentSphere does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

Any updates to this Privacy Policy will be released with future versions of the app.
